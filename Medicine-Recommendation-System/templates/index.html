Help & Instructions

How to Use the Health Care Center

Entering Symptoms

Type your symptoms in the input field or use the voice input button to describe how you're feeling.

Example symptoms: fever, headache, cough, fatigue, etc.

Voice Input

Click the "Voice Input" button and clearly describe your symptoms. The system will transcribe your words automatically.

Understanding Results

After analysis, you'll see details about the potential condition including:

  • Disease information
  • Description and symptoms
  • Recommended precautions
  • Potential medications
  • Exercise and dietary suggestions
Important: This tool provides preliminary information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
